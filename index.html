R.M. GAYLER

A Science Fiction Author

I write science fiction where technology, humanity, and the unknown collide. My stories explore the consequences of AI, the nature of consciousness, and the complexities of human relationships. From AI-driven global catastrophes to alien intelligences, I craft gripping tales that challenge our understanding of the future and what it means to be human.


Drawing inspiration from classic myths and modern science, I aim to push the boundaries of imagination while grounding my narratives in the emotional truths that shape us all. My characters face not only external threats but also deep personal dilemmas, navigating a world where ethical choices and technological progress are often at odds. Through these stories, I invite readers to embark on thought-provoking journeys with me.
